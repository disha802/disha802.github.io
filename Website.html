Mann-ki-Baat Analytics & Stock Prediction

November 2025 | Research Project
A sophisticated NLP and time-series analysis project correlating policy announcements with market movements to predict sectoral stock trends.

Research Objectives

  • Perform comprehensive sentiment analysis and topic modeling on Prime Minister's Mann-ki-Baat speeches
  • Analyze correlations between policy sentiment and subsequent market movements
  • Build predictive models for sectoral stock trends based on policy announcements
  • Achieve 78% accuracy in identifying policy-driven market impacts

Methodology

  • NLP Analysis: Used BERT (Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers) for deep contextual understanding of policy speeches
  • Topic Modeling: Applied Latent Dirichlet Allocation (LDA) to extract key policy themes and priorities
  • Sentiment Extraction: Quantified positive/negative sentiment around specific sectors mentioned in speeches
  • Time Series Analysis: Correlated sentiment metrics with stock market data across multiple time windows
  • Predictive Modeling: Built ensemble models combining NLP features with traditional financial indicators

Key Findings

  • Strong correlation between positive policy mentions and short-term sectoral rallies
  • Achieved 78% accuracy in predicting market direction for policy-affected sectors
  • Identified lag patterns between policy announcements and market responses
  • Discovered sector-specific sensitivity to policy sentiment
